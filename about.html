Our Story

Design, Planning & Construction — One Collaborative Process

Gable & Grace is a residential design-build company specializing in custom new homes, thoughtful renovations, and wellness spaces throughout Raleigh, North Carolina, and the surrounding Triangle area.

As a Raleigh custom home builder, we create beautiful, timeless homes designed around the way our clients live. Our team brings design, planning, and construction together through one collaborative process, giving homeowners a more seamless experience from the first concept through the final walkthrough.

Verified With Intention

Our Quality-Control & Testing Process

We approach every home as though it were our own. Our proprietary quality-control and testing process helps verify moisture protection, air quality, installation standards, and contractor workmanship throughout construction.

Moisture Protection

Verified building envelope and moisture-management practices to protect the long-term health of your home.

Air Quality

Independent checks on ventilation, materials, and finishes that contribute to healthier indoor air.

Workmanship

Installation standards and contractor workmanship are reviewed throughout construction, not just at the end.

Our Services

Custom Homes, Renovations & Wellness Spaces

Custom New Homes

Architecturally considered homes featuring functional floor plans, enduring materials, quality craftsmanship, and personalized details.

Home Re-Designs & Remodels
